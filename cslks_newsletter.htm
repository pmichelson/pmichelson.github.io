The C. S. Lewis and Kindred Spirits Newsletter

Updated: 1 v 2022

The CSLKS Newsletter is a periodic news and information bulletin published usually three times a year by the C. S. Lewis and Kindred Spirits for Central and Eastern Europe of Iași, Romania. It is devoted to the discussion and coverage of work of the C. S. Lewis Project of the Agora Christi Foundation of Iași and the Department of English at the Universitatea Alexandru Ioan Cuza din Iași. It provides updates and information on the work of this project as well as materials of interest to people interested in the C. S. Lewis and his Inklings circle.
